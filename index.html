Our Services

Welcome to Splashmates Swim School: Where Confidence Meets the Water!
This is what we do!

At Splashmates, we’re more than just a swim school—we’re a family-run aquatic haven where water enthusiasts of all ages immerse themselves in a sea of learning and fun. With 18 years of experience, our passion for teaching swimming knows no bounds. Whether you’re a curious toddler, an adventurous teen, or an adult seeking aquatic mastery, we’ve got you covered.

We foster a nurturing environment where every student feels at ease, building not only swimming skills but also water confidence. Our certified instructors bring expertise, patience, and a genuine love for the water to each lesson.

Swim Lessons for All Ages:

Infants (6 months to 36 months): Join our delightful Moms and Tots classes, where caregivers participate alongside their little ones.
Children and Teens: Age-appropriate classes tailored to individual swimming capabilities.
Adults: It’s never too late to learn! Dive in and conquer the water with our expert guidance.

Schedule and availability:

Twice a Week: Lessons on Mondays/Wednesdays or Tuesdays/Thursdays.
20-30 Minute Sessions: Indoors or outdoors, tailored to your needs.
Summer Months: Splash in our outdoor and indoor pools, basking in the sun.
Winter Months: Warm up in our heated indoor pool, ensuring year-round aquatic enjoyment.

About Us

Our Journey

Splashmates Swim School was born from a shared love for water and a vision to create a nurturing space where swimmers of all ages could thrive. Founded 18 years ago by Claudia, a passionate swim instructor, our school has become a beacon for aquatic enthusiasts in the heart of Gauteng, South Africa.

We believe that swimming is more than a skill—it’s a life-saving art and a source of joy. Our teaching philosophy revolves around three core principles:
Safety First: We prioritize safety above all else. Whether it’s teaching water survival skills to infants or refining strokes for advanced swimmers, safety remains our compass.
Individualized Approach: No two swimmers are alike. We tailor our lessons to each student’s needs, abilities, and comfort level. Whether you’re overcoming fear or aiming for competitive excellence, we’ve got a personalized plan for you.
Building Confidence: Water can be intimidating, especially for beginners. Our instructors create a supportive environment where confidence blossoms. We celebrate every milestone, from the first float to the perfect butterfly kick.
